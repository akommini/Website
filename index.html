I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering supervised by Prof. Zlatan Aksamija at Umass, Amherst. Broadly my research is to build predictive models coupled with high performance cloud computing tools to simulate probabilistic systems. In particular, my focus is to understand the charge transport dynamics in semiconductors to evaluate their thermoelectric capabilities.
